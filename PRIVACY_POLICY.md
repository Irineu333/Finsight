# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **Finsight** ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information.

## 1. Summary

Finsight is a personal finance management app. All data you enter is stored **locally on your device only**. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

## 2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any of the following:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Financial account credentials or bank data
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Any data transmitted to external servers

## 3. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data **exclusively on your device**:

- Financial records you manually enter (transactions, categories, installments)
- App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Finsight does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms that collect user data.

## 5. Data Deletion

To delete all data associated with the app, simply uninstall it from your device. All locally stored data will be permanently removed.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Finsight does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not collect any personal information from any user.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
